## Company Identity
Zwitch is an Indian fintech infrastructure platform that provides API-based
banking and payment solutions for businesses and fintech companies.

It enables companies to integrate financial services into their products
without directly managing banking partnerships or regulatory complexity.

## Core Focus Areas
- Banking-as-a-Service (BaaS)
- Payment and payout APIs
- Embedded finance infrastructure
- Developer-first fintech APIs

## Target Customers
- Fintech startups
- SaaS companies
- Marketplaces
- Businesses requiring embedded financial services

## Primary Capabilities (High-Level)
- **Payment Gateway**: Full-featured payment gateway with 150+ payment methods, payment links, instant refunds, and recurring payments
- API-driven banking infrastructure
- Payment processing and payouts
- Virtual accounts
- Partner bank integrations
- Compliance-ready fintech workflows

## Product Categories
Zwitch offers four main product categories:
1. **Payment Gateway**: Accept online payments with 150+ payment options, payment links, instant refunds, recurring payments, and native SDKs (Android, iOS, Flutter, Layer.js)
2. **Payouts**: Connected Banking with 150+ banks for instant account-to-account transfers (NEFT/RTGS/IMPS/UPI), escrow payments
3. **Zwitch Bill Connect**: Interoperable bill payments connecting 1000+ ERPs and banks via NPCI's Bharat Connect Network, Instant Bill Discounting API, API Marketplace
4. **Verification Suite**: Verification APIs (VPA, Bank Account, PAN, Name), Compliance APIs, Onboarding APIs

For detailed product information, see [Products Overview](./products_overview.md).

## What Zwitch Is NOT
- Not a licensed bank
- Not a consumer-facing financial app
- Not a crypto or blockchain-native platform (unless explicitly stated elsewhere)

> **Note**: While Zwitch offers a full Payment Gateway solution for businesses, it is not a standalone consumer payment gateway for individual end-users. It's designed for businesses to integrate payment capabilities into their applications.
