## Overview

Open Money leverages transactional data and business relationships to provide alternative lending products for businesses. These solutions enable businesses to access financing based on their transaction history, revenue patterns, and relationships with anchor companies.

## Lending Philosophy

### Data-Driven Lending

Open Money uses:
- **Transaction History**: Rich transaction data for credit assessment
- **Revenue Patterns**: Revenue trends and patterns for risk assessment
- **Business Relationships**: Anchor company relationships for financing
- **Cash Flow Analysis**: Cash flow patterns for credit decisions

### Alternative to Traditional Lending

Open Money's lending products provide:
- **Faster Approval**: Quick approval based on transaction data
- **Minimal Paperwork**: Reduced documentation requirements
- **Flexible Terms**: Flexible repayment terms based on business needs
- **Competitive Rates**: Competitive interest rates based on risk assessment
