## Overview

Open Payroll is Open Money's comprehensive HR and payroll management system for employee compensation, compliance, and HR operations. It enables businesses to automate payroll processing, ensure statutory compliance, and manage employee lifecycle.

## Core Payroll Features

### Automated Payroll Calculation

- **Salary Calculation:** Automatic calculation of salaries with all components
- **TDS Calculations:** Automatic TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) calculations
- **Deductions:** Automatic deduction of taxes, provident fund, and other statutory deductions
- **Allowances:** Support for various allowances (HRA, LTA, medical, etc.)
- **Overtime:** Automatic calculation of overtime payments
- **Bonus and Incentives:** Support for variable pay components

### Payslip Generation

- **100% Statutory Compliant:** Auto-generate 100% statutory compliant payslips
- **Multiple Formats:** Generate payslips in various formats (digital, PDF, etc.)
- **Customizable Templates:** Use custom templates for payslip design
- **Multi-Language Support:** Support for multiple languages
- **Digital Distribution:** Automatic email distribution to employees
- **Employee Self-Service:** Employees can view and download payslips

### One-Click Processing

- **Monthly Payroll:** Run monthly payroll in a single click
- **Bulk Processing:** Process payroll for all employees simultaneously
- **Automated Calculations:** All calculations done automatically
- **Error Checking:** Automatic validation and error detection
- **Approval Workflow:** Configurable approval workflows

### Form 16 Generation

- **Automatic Preparation:** Automatic Form 16 preparation for tax filing
- **Tax Compliance:** Ensures compliance with income tax regulations
- **Digital Distribution:** Automatic distribution to employees
- **Multiple Formats:** Support for various formats

### Salary Reports

- **Comprehensive Reports:** Detailed salary and compensation reports
- **Analytics:** Payroll analytics and insights
- **Cost Analysis:** Employee cost analysis and budgeting
- **Trend Analysis:** Historical payroll trends
