## Overview

Open Money offers comprehensive expense management solutions through VISA-powered corporate cards, enabling businesses to control spending, track expenses, and streamline reimbursement processes.

## Corporate Card Features

### VISA-Powered Cards

- **Secure, Internationally Recognized:** VISA-backed corporate cards accepted globally
- **Digital & Physical Options:** Both digital and physical card issuance available
- **Smart Controls:** Set spending limits and approve transactions in real-time
- **Expense Tracking:** Comprehensive tracking of all employee spending
- **Real-Time Approval:** Managers can approve or reject expenses instantly
- **Reconciliation Tools:** Automatic reconciliation of card expenses
- **No Receipt Requirements:** Prepaid expense cards that eliminate receipt saving burden

## Card Types Offered

### 1. Standard Corporate Cards

**For general business expenses and vendor payments**

Features:
- Standard spending limits
- Real-time transaction monitoring
- Automatic expense categorization
- Integration with accounting software

### 2. Prepaid Expense Cards

**Physical cards for employees making offline purchases**

- No receipt requirements

- Automatic expense recording

- Field operations
- Offline purchases
- Cashless expense management

### 3. Travel & Entertainment Cards

**Dedicated cards for business travel and dining expenses**

Features:
- Travel-specific spending limits
