# Open Money — Banking Solutions for Banks

## Overview

Open Money offers **white-label platform solutions** for banks to enhance their business banking offerings. Banks can leverage Open Money's **platform/dashboard** to provide comprehensive business payment and financial management solutions to their customers, all within the bank's own banking platform.

**Note:** Open Money provides a **platform/dashboard interface**, not direct APIs. Banks integrate Open Money's platform features into their own platforms through white-label solutions.

## Partnership Model

### Bank Partnership Services

Open Money partners with **15 out of 20 top Indian banks** to provide:

- **White-Label Platform Solutions**: Banks can offer Open Money's platform features under their own brand
- **Platform Integration**: Banks can integrate Open Money's dashboard/platform into their existing platforms
- **Co-Branded Solutions**: Banks and Open Money can co-brand platform solutions for their customers
- **Infrastructure Sharing**: Banks leverage Open Money's platform infrastructure for business payments

### Value Proposition for Banks

Banks benefit from:
- **Enhanced Customer Experience**: Comprehensive business payment solutions
- **Reduced Development Costs**: Leverage existing Open Money infrastructure
- **Faster Time to Market**: Quick deployment of business payment features
- **Competitive Advantage**: Offer modern business banking solutions
- **Customer Retention**: Better tools help retain business customers

## Core Solutions

### 1. Payments & Collections Platform

**Help customers pay vendors easily, accept online payments, and facilitate bill and invoice reconciliation — all within the bank's own banking platform.**

#### Features for Bank Customers:

- **Vendor Payments**:
  - Bill management and payment processing
  - Direct account-to-account payments
  - Bulk payment capabilities
  - Payment tracking and status updates

- **Payment Collections**:
  - GST-compliant invoicing
  - Payment links for easy collection
  - Multiple payment methods (net banking, UPI, cards)
  - Instant settlement to bank accounts

- **Reconciliation**:
  - Automatic bill and invoice reconciliation
  - Two-way sync with accounting software
  - Payment matching and verification
  - Discrepancy detection and alerts

#### Integration:

- Seamlessly integrated into bank's existing platform
- Single sign-on with bank credentials
- Unified dashboard for all banking and payment operations
- Real-time transaction visibility

### 2. Cards & Expense Management

**Issue VISA-powered expense cards, smart budgeting and control features, real-time spend tracking and approval, employee reimbursement automation, and budget enforcement at department or project level.**

#### Features for Bank Customers:

- **Corporate Cards**:
  - VISA-powered corporate cards
  - Digital and physical card options
  - Smart controls and spending limits
  - Real-time expense tracking

- **Expense Management**:
  - Automatic expense categorization
  - Receipt management
  - Approval workflows
  - Expense reports and analytics

- **Budget Controls**:
  - Department-level budgets
  - Project-based spending limits
  - Real-time budget alerts
  - Budget enforcement

- **Employee Reimbursement**:
  - Automated reimbursement processing
  - Prepaid expense cards
  - No receipt requirements
  - Direct salary integration

#### Integration:

- Card issuance through bank's existing card infrastructure
- Expense data integrated with bank statements
- Unified expense and banking dashboard

### 4. Alternative Lending Products

**Leverage business relationships and transaction data to provide financing solutions:**

#### Anchor-Based Financing

- **Description**: Businesses with strong relationships with anchor companies can access financing at competitive rates
- **Use Case**: Distribution networks, supply chain financing
- **Benefits**: 
  - Leverage anchor company relationships
  - Competitive interest rates
  - Minimal paperwork
  - Scalable solution for channel partners

#### Revenue-Based Financing

- **Description**: Financing based on business revenue and cash flows
- **Use Case**: Growing businesses with consistent revenue
- **Benefits**:
  - Flexible repayment based on revenue
  - No fixed monthly payments
  - Access to capital without equity dilution

#### Early Settlement Programs

- **Description**: Options to get paid early by suppliers for a discount
- **Use Case**: Businesses needing faster cash flow
- **Benefits**:
  - Faster access to funds
  - Improved cash flow management
  - Flexible settlement terms

#### Working Capital Loans

- **Description**: Short-term loans for operational needs
- **Use Case**: Seasonal businesses, inventory management
- **Benefits**:
  - Quick access to working capital
  - Flexible repayment terms
  - Based on transaction history

#### Business Credit Cards

- **Description**: Specialized credit cards for SME business needs
- **Use Case**: Business expenses, vendor payments
- **Benefits**:
  - Credit facility for business expenses
  - Rewards and benefits
  - Expense tracking and management

## Implementation Model

### White-Label Platform Integration

Banks can:
- **Brand Customization**: Customize UI/UX to match bank's brand
- **Platform Integration**: Integrate Open Money's dashboard/platform into existing bank platforms
- **Single Sign-On**: Use bank's authentication system
- **Unified Experience**: Seamless experience for bank customers
- **Feature Access**: Provide access to all Open Money platform features through bank's interface

### Platform Integration

Banks can:
- **Dashboard Integration**: Integrate Open Money's dashboard features into bank's platform
- **Selective Features**: Choose which platform features to offer
- **Custom Workflows**: Configure workflows using Open Money platform features
- **Data Integration**: Integrate Open Money platform data with bank systems

**Note:** For API access, banks would need to use Zwitch APIs, not Open Money APIs (as Open Money doesn't provide APIs directly).

Banks and Open Money can:
- **Joint Branding**: Co-brand solutions for customers
- **Shared Infrastructure**: Leverage both bank and Open Money infrastructure
- **Customer Support**: Joint customer support model
- **Marketing**: Collaborative marketing and customer acquisition
