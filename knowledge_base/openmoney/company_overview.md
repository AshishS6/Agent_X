## Company Identity
Open Money is an Indian **fintech operating platform** that provides a
**dashboard and mobile app** for businesses to manage their banking, payments,
and financial operations.

It focuses on providing a **user-facing platform** (web dashboard and mobile app)
where businesses can access all financial management features including connected
banking, invoicing, payment links, bill management, expense management, payroll,
and GST compliance.

**Important:** Open Money is a **platform/dashboard** for businesses, not an API provider.
APIs for payment processing and banking services are provided by **Zwitch**, not Open Money.

## Core Focus Areas
- **Platform/Dashboard Interface**: Web dashboard and mobile app for financial management
- **Connected Banking**: Connect and manage bank accounts through the platform
- **Business Payments and Collections**: Invoice and payment management through the dashboard
- **Financial Operations**: Comprehensive financial management for SMEs and startups
- **User-Facing Solutions**: Easy-to-use platform for business owners and finance teams

## Target Customers
- Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs)
- Startups
- Fintech companies
- Digital-first businesses operating in India

## Primary Capabilities (High-Level)
- **Connected Banking**: Connect current accounts from ICICI, SBI, Axis, Yes Bank
- **Pay Vendors**: Bill management and direct account-to-account payments
- **Get Paid**: GST-compliant invoices, payment links, multiple payment modes
- **Auto-Reconciliation**: Two-way sync with accounting software (Tally, Zoho Books, Oracle NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics)
- **GST Compliance**: Invoicing, tax filing, compliance workflows
- Virtual accounts
- Financial workflow automation
- Built-in integrations with accounting software and banks (no API required from users)

For detailed product information, see [Products Overview](./products_overview.md).

## What Open Money Is NOT
- Not a consumer banking application
- Not a personal finance or wallet app
- Not a neobank for individuals
- Not a cryptocurrency or Web3 platform (unless explicitly stated elsewhere)

## Operating Geography
- Primarily focused on India

## Regulatory Context
- **Payment Aggregator (PA) licensed by RBI** - Open Money is a licensed Payment Aggregator under Reserve Bank of India regulations
- **Settlement Liability**: As a licensed PA, Open Money is liable for settlements and transaction processing
- Operates within Indian financial regulations
- Subject to RBI guidelines and compliance requirements
- Supports KYC and AML-compliant workflows for businesses

## Platform Statistics
- **3.5 Million+** businesses using Open Money
- **$35 Billion+** transactions processed annually
- **65,000+** tax practitioners
- **15 out of 20** top banks use Open
- **Major bank partnerships**: ICICI Bank, State Bank of India, Axis Bank, Yes Bank

## Accounting Software Integrations
- **Tally**: Full integration with Tally accounting software
- **Zoho Books**: Seamless sync with Zoho Books
- **Oracle NetSuite**: Enterprise-level integration
- **Microsoft Dynamics**: Microsoft Dynamics integration

## Positioning Summary
Open Money positions itself as a fintech infrastructure layer that helps
businesses build, manage, and scale financial operations without directly
becoming a licensed bank themselves.
